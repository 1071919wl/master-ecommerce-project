Bacon Turkey Avocado Sandwich

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients:

  • Wheat bread
    • 2 slices toasted
  • Advocado
    • 1/2 of an avocado
  • Turkey
    • 1/4 lb of sliced turkey
  • Bacon
    • 4 pieces of crispy apple-wood smoked
  • Honey Mustard BBQ Sauce
    • 2 tablespoon spread
  • Lettuce (optional)
    • 1 leaf

Description:

Great for lazy single dudes who just needs substances to survive. You will need bread of you choice (at least two slices), advocado (to make it fancy), turkey, bacon, leftover honey mustard bbq sauce from Chick-fil-a and lettuce (if you like rabbit food). The only recipe you need for the rest of your life (may also impress unexpected guests).

Directions:

  1. Toast bread until desired toastness.
  2. Pan fry or bake bacon strips.
  3. Spread honey mustard bbq sauce on bread.
  4. Use 1/2 advocado to lay on bread.
  5. Assemble the incredients and enjoy.
